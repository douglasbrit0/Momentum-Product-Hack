Comparative Study: UniSync's Old vs New UI

Company: UniSync

Overview

UniSync is an app designed to connect university students, functioning similarly to LinkedIn but specifically tailored for the college community. This study provides a detailed comparative analysis of the old and new user interfaces (UI) of UniSync.

Study Objectives

The primary objective of this study is to evaluate usability, visual aesthetics, and overall user satisfaction between the two UI designs. The study investigates:

  • The ease of navigation in the old vs new UI.
  • Visual improvements and design trends incorporated in the new UI.
  • User performance and satisfaction levels during typical tasks.

Methodology

The study involved a mixed-methods approach including user surveys, A/B testing, and direct observation sessions. Participants were asked to complete tasks on both the old and new interfaces and provide feedback on aspects such as usability, visual appeal, and overall experience.

Preliminary Findings

Early feedback indicates that the new UI offers a cleaner, more modern appearance which simplifies navigation, though some users noted a learning curve when transitioning from the old interface. Detailed statistical analysis and further user feedback will continue to be collected in the coming months.
